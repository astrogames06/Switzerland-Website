History

  1. 1291. Switzerland began with three regions. These regions were Uri, Schwyz and Unterwalden.
  2. 1315. The Battle of Morgarten. Switzerland was invaded by the Habsburgs from Austria. Miraculously they where able to win the battle.
  3. 1506. Establishment of the Swiss Guards. The swiss guards were created to protect the Pope in rome. They where known for the loyalty and bravery.
  4. 16th Century. Reformation and Wars of Kappel. Some swiss people wanted to change the way the worshiped but other disagreed. This led to conflicts and fights which in turn created different areas of Switzerland.
  5. 1815. Congress of Vienna. After a big war, leaders around Europe came together and decided that Switzerland should be left alone as its own country.
  6. 19th Century. Switzerland's Neutrality. Switzerland didnt want to get involved in other countries conflicts so it decided to stay neutral.
  7. 1863. Establishment of the Red Cross. After a man named Henry Dunant started the Red Cross, an organisation that helps people during wars and natural disasters. He started after seeing injured soilders on a battle field getting no help.
  8. 20th Century. Switzerland made a big change. They introducted something called the "direct democracy" which allowed citizens to vote on important issues.
